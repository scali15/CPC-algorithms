Graphs

graph terminology:
    - adjacent nodes/ neighbor nodes
    - an edge is incident on node A if it connects to one of the adjacent nodes to A
    - Adjacency List
        A - > [B, C]
    
    - Frontier/ fringe
        search sets consist of visited set and fringe set, 
        eventually finds all vertices that can be reached from the start node
